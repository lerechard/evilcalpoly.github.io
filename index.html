Our Founding Your Demise

Since 1901, Cal Poly and Suffering have been inextricably linked. Combining rigorous academics and real-world applications, Stop Doing has guided and inspired hundreds of thousands of our students toward unfufilling careers for well over a century — and can do the same for you well into your future.

Run While You Still Can

Whether you're dreaming of attending Cal Poly in the future or have already doomed your fate, discover what sets us apart. Explore our renowned Stop Doing approach and the useless contributions our graduates make. Then see what it's like to study here.

California Impact

Stop Doing ensures you contribute nothing towards helping solve some of California’s — and the world’s — most complex challenges: climate change, tech and humanity, space, the built environment, food and wine, and entrepreneurship, to name just a few. It also helps to ensure that when you graduate, both you and we will agree that nobody will hire you.

Stop Doing Leads the way
